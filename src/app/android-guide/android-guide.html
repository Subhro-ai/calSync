Add to Google Calendar (Android)

1.

Copy the Link

First, copy the subscription link you generated on the previous page. You will need to do the next steps on a computer.

2.

Open Google Calendar

On your computer, go to calendar.google.com. On the left side, find "Other calendars" and click the plus (+) sign.

3.

Add From URL

Select "From URL" from the menu. Paste your subscription link into the field and click "Add calendar". The calendar will automatically appear in your Google Calendar app on Android.
